Welcome to UNCG's guide for food close by!

Are you a culinary adventurer in search of the perfect meal on or near campus? Look no further! We’ve scoured the area around the Petty building to bring you the top three food establishments that are a must-visit for any food lover.

Whether you’re craving a quick lunch, or a delicious dinner, this list has something to satisfy every palate. So grab your wallet and walking shoes and get ready to explore the best flavors our campus has to offer!

1. Chick-fil-A

Located just a short walk from the Petty building, Chick-fil-A offers delicious food fast and for a reasonable price.

2. Yum Yum Better Ice Cream

Craving ice cream? Maybe even some cheap and tasty hot dogs? Well, look no further! Yum Yum's has some of the best value for food on campus—you can get hot dogs and ice cream all for less than $10.

3. Slices Pizza by Tony

Well if you are like me you could eat pizza for every meal, so i had to include this restaurant. Located south-east of the petty building the slightly farther walk is worth it for the mouth-watering pizza and subs.
